About Us

Current Projects

Embark on scientific exploration at the Central Research Lab. Our projects in chemical and life sciences include drug discovery, genomics, and more. With a multidisciplinary approach and cutting-edge tools, we pioneer treatments and unearth insights. Join our journey of innovation and discovery.

Vacinnology

The Central Research Lab leads in vaccinology, covering vaccine development, immunology, clinical trials, and epidemiology. We create safe vaccines with global health impact through biotech and innovation. We combat vaccine hesitancy and promote equitable distribution. Our dedication to cutting-edge research advances vaccine science for better global health..

Microbiology

TThe Central Research Lab is a microbiology hub, spanning infectious diseases, biotechnology, and more. We explore microorganisms' impact on health and the environment, advancing science for societal betterment.

Cell Science

The Central Research Laboratory conducts cutting-edge cell science research, delving into cell structure, function, and signaling. Our work spans diverse areas, contributing to fundamental biological understanding and its applications in medicine and biotechnology..
